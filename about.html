About Phathu Academy

Welcome to Phathu Academy, an online learning platform dedicated to helping Grade 10, Grade 11 and Grade 12 learners master Mathematics and Physical Sciences.

Our mission is to make quality education accessible, engaging and affordable for every learner. We believe every student can succeed with the right guidance, consistent practice and clear explanations.

Through video lessons, downloadable study notes, quizzes, interactive simulations and AI-powered learning tools, we aim to make learning easier and enjoyable.

Our Vision

To become one of Africa's leading online education platforms, empowering learners through innovative technology and world-class teaching.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality Mathematics and Physical Sciences education that helps students understand concepts, achieve excellent results and prepare for future careers.
